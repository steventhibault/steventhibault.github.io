Compliance Reporting Case Study

Compliance reporting automation for messy operational data.

A Stephen Thibault case study about compliance reporting automation, operational reporting, spreadsheet cleanup, recurring calculations, and audit-ready summaries.

Problem

Operational and compliance reporting often depends on scattered spreadsheets, manually exported reports, recurring calculations, and source files that were not originally designed to work together.

Solution approach

The reporting workflow can be rebuilt around repeatable data cleanup, clear assumptions, protected formulas, validation checks, and outputs that are easier to review.

Reporting improvements

  • Combine recurring source files into a cleaner reporting structure.
  • Rebuild formulas and summaries so calculations are easier to verify.
  • Improve print-ready or PDF-ready outputs.
  • Track exceptions, missing data, and unusual results.
  • Make reporting easier to update for the next period.

Operational value

The goal is a reporting process that is less fragile, easier to explain, easier to repeat, and more useful for operational decisions.
