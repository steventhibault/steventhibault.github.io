Healthcare Onboarding Case Study

Healthcare onboarding automation for document-heavy hiring workflows.

A Stephen Thibault case study about improving healthcare onboarding workflows, hiring packets, compliance documents, training records, and folder organization.

Problem

Hiring and onboarding workflows can include many forms, acknowledgments, background-check documents, training records, payroll forms, and required signatures. When those files are scanned and organized manually, the process can become slow and inconsistent.

Solution approach

The improved workflow focuses on separating, naming, routing, checking, and tracking onboarding records in a more repeatable way. Staff still review important exceptions, but the repeated organization work becomes easier to manage.

Workflow components

  • Document checklist structure for expected onboarding files.
  • Consistent naming and folder placement.
  • Review points for missing or uncertain documents.
  • Support for training and competency-test workflows.
  • Operational reporting for compliance visibility.

Operational value

A cleaner onboarding workflow reduces repetitive administrative effort and makes it easier to see which records are complete, which items need follow-up, and where the process is getting stuck.
