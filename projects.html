filepackR

filepackR is PDF document-processing software designed to turn mixed files into cleaner, organized, audit-ready records. It combines PDF splitting, merging, OCR-powered recognition, customizable rules, and Intelligent File Routing to move documents through the right workflows.

  • Split mixed PDF packets into separate documents
  • Merge related pages and supporting records
  • Apply consistent filenames and routing rules
  • Surface review items instead of silently guessing
  • Support local-first processing for sensitive documents
