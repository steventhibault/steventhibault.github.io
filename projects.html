filepackR: PDF document processing powered by Intelligent File Routing.

A custom software solution designed around a real operational workflow: sorting, reviewing, auditing, and routing document-heavy packets more efficiently.

Click to view full size

Cleaner document workflows with less repetitive work.

FilePackR is scanned paperwork automation software that turns mixed stacks of paper into organized, workflow-ready PDF files. After users scan documents from a desktop scanner, FilePackR uses OCR, customizable rules, and review queues to identify each document, split or combine pages when needed, name the files, route them to the correct destination, and flag anything that needs review. This can save companies hundreds of hours in manual labor.

  • ✓Split scanned PDF packets into separate documents
  • ✓Merge related pages and supporting records
  • ✓Apply consistent filenames and routing rules
  • ✓Flag review items instead of silently guessing
  • ✓Support local-first processing for sensitive files

Custom configure-to-order workflow built around component choices, quote items, and a live configuration summary.

Migrating a 20,000-SKU Magento catalog to Shopify while preserving a complex configure-to-order workflow.

A computer hardware distributor needed to move its storefront from Magento to Shopify. A basic catalog migration would have been simple enough, but customers were not just buying individual products. They needed to configure refurbished servers piece by piece, with sections for chassis, drives, processors, memory, power supplies, warranties, quote items, and other build options.

The project combined platform migration, catalog cleanup, integration support, and custom configuration logic. The result was a Shopify storefront that could support a large SKU catalog, preserve SEO-sensitive content and URL planning, and keep the configure-to-order buying process familiar for customers and manageable for non-technical staff.

  • ✓Migrated a 20,000+ SKU catalog from Magento to Shopify
  • ✓Rebuilt the configure-to-order server workflow inside Shopify
  • ✓Supported component sections, quote items, cart flow, and current-configuration tracking
  • ✓Maintained payment, inventory, and SEO-sensitive migration requirements
  • ✓Gave non-technical staff more control without constant developer changes
Case study Compliance Reporting Reconcile. Report. Attest.

Turning fragmented compliance data into a single audit-ready report.

A regional home healthcare provider needed to produce quarterly compliance reports pulling from electronic visit verification (EVV) data, participant records, and several separate operational logs, previously a manual reconciliation process repeated by hand every quarter.

Built a consolidated reporting workbook that integrates EVV data, participant demographics and health-condition tracking, discharge and transportation logs, and a financial summary into one system, with automated formulas replacing manual cross-referencing and a structured attestation process for audit readiness.

  • ✓Eliminated manual reconciliation across separate data sources
  • ✓Automated compliance calculations across all four quarters
  • ✓Financial summary tied directly to source data
  • ✓Reduced a multi-day manual process to a repeatable system
Case study Visit Reconciliation Territory. Client. Exception.

Reconciling multi-state operational data at the territory and client level.

A home healthcare operation running across multiple states needed clearer visibility into visit-level data by territory and by client, with reconciliation in place to catch discrepancies before they became compliance issues.

Built a comparison workbook that processes raw visit data into weekly territory and client-level views, with dedicated reconciliation logic and state-specific reporting, surfacing exceptions instead of requiring manual review of every record.

  • ✓Weekly rollups by territory and by client from raw visit data
  • ✓State-specific reconciliation views for multi-state operations
  • ✓Exception-based review instead of full manual audits
